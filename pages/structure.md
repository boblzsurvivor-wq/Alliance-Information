# {{ site.alliance_name }} Alliance Structure

---

## OVERVIEW

{{ site.alliance_name }} operates through a role based structure.

Each role exists to ensure the alliance runs efficiently, grows consistently, and performs well in events.

---

## R5 COMMANDER

The R5 is responsible for:

- Final decision making  
- War authorization  
- Diplomacy  
- Officer management  
- Emergency actions  

The R5 provides direction for the entire alliance.

---

## R4 OFFICERS

Officers manage execution, coordination, and enforcement.

---

### ⚔️ WARFARE

**Warlord**

- Leads wars in real time  
- Decides attacks and rallies  
- Gives commands  

**War Strategist**

- Plans events and wars  
- Develops strategies  
- Supports the Warlord  

---

### 🏗 DEVELOPMENT

**Logistics Planner**

- Sets development strategy  
- Prioritizes technology  
- Optimizes preparation  

**Training Officer**

- Sets goals  
- Tracks progress  
- Manages performance  

---

### 🧠 INTELLIGENCE & CONTROL

**Intelligence Officer**

- Tracks enemies  
- Analyzes data  
- Supports strategy  

**Security Officer**

- Enforces rules  
- Monitors activity  
- Manages removals  

---

### 🤝 COMMUNITY

**Member Support**

- Helps members  
- Answers questions  
- Maintains morale  

**Recruitment**

- Finds and vets players  
- Manages new members  

---

## OFFICER AUTHORITY

Officers may:

- Enforce rules  
- Issue warnings  
- Coordinate operations  

Officers may not override R5 decisions.
