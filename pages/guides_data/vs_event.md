# VS Weekly Command Guide

A structured weekly plan for Alliance Duel (VS), designed for leadership execution and member alignment.

---

## 🌿 Sunday – Preparation Day

**Reset day. Prepare properly — this sets the tone for the entire week.**

### Mandatory Saves (Monday)
- Radar Missions  
- Blueprints  
- Wrenches  

### Hold for Weekly Rotation
- Accelerators (Tuesday, Wednesday, Friday)  
- Recruitment items (Tuesday)  
- Pulse Modules & Forging Stones (Tuesday)  
- Hero Fragments & Power Cores (Thursday)  

### Required Actions
- Stack research and construction near completion (do NOT finish)  
- Gather and restock all resources  
- Heal troops and return to full strength  

### Weekly Plan
- **Monday:** Radar, Blueprints, Wrenches + full gathering  
- **Tuesday:** Building push + refugees + modules/stones  
- **Wednesday:** Research focus (Badge priority) + trucks  
- **Thursday:** Hero upgrades (fragments, cores, gear)  
- **Friday:** Training, upgrades, radar, healing speed-ups  
- **Saturday:** Combat push — bounties, trucks, SVS, farm RSS  

---

## 🔧 Monday – Mod Vehicle Boost

**Strong start required. Use saved items and maintain activity.**

### Priority
- Complete Radar Missions  
- Use Blueprints  
- Use Wrenches  
- Gather resources (prioritize Zents)  

### Activity Requirement
- Keep marches gathering at all times  
- Use gathering boosts where available  

### Prep for Tuesday
- Stack construction near completion (do NOT finish)  
- Save building speed-ups  
- Stockpile building resources  
- Save recruitment items, modules, and stones  

---

## 🏠 Tuesday – Shelter Upgrade

**Primary power gain day. Push buildings aggressively.**

### Priority
- Upgrade buildings (focus high-value targets)  
- Use building speed-ups  
- Recruit Refugees  
- Use Pulse Modules  
- Use Forging Stones  
- Complete Orange (Tier S) Bounty Missions  

### Execution
- Prioritize HQ, military, and key buildings  
- Keep builders active at all times  
- Use accelerators efficiently  

### Prep for Wednesday
- Stack research near completion (do NOT finish)  
- Save research speed-ups  
- Preserve tech materials  

---

## 🔬 Wednesday – Age of Science

**Research focus. Maximise technology gains.**

### Priority
- Complete research upgrades  
- Use research speed-ups  
- Focus Badge Research  
- Send Orange (Tier S) Trucks  

### Execution
- Keep research active at all times  
- Prioritize military and badge tech  
- Use accelerators efficiently  

### Prep for Thursday
- Save Hero Fragments  
- Save Power Cores  
- Save Alloys  
- Save Prime Recruitment Tickets  
- Save Equipment Fragments  

---

## 🧬 Thursday – Hero Initiative

**Hero power day. Focus upgrades for maximum impact.**

### Priority
- Use Hero Fragments  
- Use Power Cores  
- Use Alloys  
- Use Prime Recruitment Tickets  
- Use Equipment Fragments  

### Execution
- Prioritize main combat heroes  
- Focus upgrades on primary march  
- Avoid spreading resources thin  

### Prep for Friday
- Save Radar Missions  
- Save accelerators  
- Stack research and construction (do NOT finish)  
- Train units ahead for collection  

---

## 🔩 Friday – Holistic Growth

**Full development push. Convert progress into points.**

### Priority
- Train troops  
- Collect pre-trained troops  
- Complete stacked research upgrades  
- Complete stacked construction upgrades  
- Complete Radar Missions  
- Use healing speed-ups  

### Execution
- Keep training queues active  
- Use accelerators where needed  
- Prioritize higher tier units  

### Prep for Saturday
- Stock healing resources  
- Prepare troops for combat  

---

## ⚔️ Saturday – Enemy Buster

**Battle day. Push aggressively and score hard.**

### Priority
- Defeat enemy troops  
- Complete Tier S Bounty Missions  
- Send Orange (Tier S) Trucks  
- Participate in SVS battles  
- Farm enemies to maximise RSS  

### Execution
- Target rival VS alliance members  
- Take efficient fights only  
- Coordinate attacks across alliance  
- Heal in batches of 100  
- Avoid unnecessary accelerator use  

---

## 🎯 Summary

Follow the weekly cycle, save strategically, and execute each day with discipline.  
Efficiency and coordination win the week.
