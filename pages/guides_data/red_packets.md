## 🎁 Red Packet Guidelines

**Goal:** Keep red packets fun, generous, and community-driven.

### ✨ Use Them to Build the Community
- Celebrate wins, milestones, and good moments  
- Encourage activity and bring people together  
- **Be creative and have fun with them**  
- Share generously and keep the vibe positive  

### 🤝 Keep It Genuine
- Give freely and with good intent  
- Let participation and decisions stay neutural  
- Focus on the enjoyment, not the outcome  

> Red packets are about boosting morale and having fun together.
