## Alliance Structure

Our alliance is organized to ensure smooth collaboration and fair opportunities for all members.

### Hierarchy

```
Alliance Leader
├── Officers
│   ├── Event Coordinator
│   ├── Recruitment Officer
│   └── Treasurer
└── Members
    ├── Active Members
    ├── New Members
    └── Inactive Members
```

### Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| **Alliance Leader** | Overall direction, major decisions, conflict resolution |
| **Officers** | Support leader, manage specific areas (events, recruitment, resources) |
| **Event Coordinator** | Organize group activities, raids, competitions |
| **Recruitment Officer** | Vet new members, maintain recruitment standards |
| **Treasurer** | Manage shared resources and alliance bank |
| **Active Members** | Regular participation, team contributions, community engagement |
| **New Members** | Learning game mechanics, following guidelines, building reputation |

## How to Participate

### Requirements to Join
- Be level 10+ in the game
- Read and agree to our [Alliance Rules](./RULES.md)
- No toxic or disruptive behavior
- Commitment to participate at least 2-3 times per week

### Steps to Join
1. **Request to Join** - Search our alliance and submit a request
2. **Get Approved** - Our Recruitment Officer will review your request
3. **Read Guidelines** - Review our rules and contribution policies
4. **Introduce Yourself** - Say hello to the community!
5. **Start Playing** - Join events and collaborate with members

### Ways to Contribute
- ✅ Participate in alliance events and raids
- ✅ Help newer members learn the game
- ✅ Share tips, strategies, and resources
- ✅ Attend scheduled alliance gatherings
- ✅ Contribute to shared objectives
