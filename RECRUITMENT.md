## Membership Requirements

### Minimum Requirements

You must meet ALL of the following:

✅ **Game Level**: Minimum Level 10
- Shows basic game understanding
- Has experience with mechanics
- Owns starter equipment

✅ **Age**: 13 years or older
- Compliance with most platform policies
- Maturity for communication
- Ability to follow complex rules

✅ **Language**: Basic English fluency
- Can understand alliance communication
- Can communicate with teammates
- Can read guides and instructions

✅ **Time Commitment**: Available 2-3 times per week
- Can participate in events
- Keeps alliance active
- Shows dedication to community

✅ **Agreement**: Must accept our rules
- Read all guidelines
- Commit to conduct standards
- Agree to our values

### Recommended (Not Required)

These enhance your experience but aren't mandatory:

- 📊 Competitive rank or high stats
- 🎮 Experience in squad play
- 🎙️ Voice chat capability
- 💬 Active on gaming forums
- 🏆 Prior alliance experience

## Application Process

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Find Our Alliance
**In Last Z, Search for Alliance**:
1. Open Last Z game
2. Go to "Alliances" section
3. Click "Search Alliance"
4. Search: `[ALLIANCE_NAME]` (ask for exact name)
5. Review alliance details

#### Step 2: Submit Application
**Application Form**:
1. Click "Apply to Join"
2. Fill out brief application:
   - Player name
   - Current level
   - Why do you want to join?
   - Experience with squad play?
   - Time availability?
3. Click "Submit"
4. Confirmation message appears

#### Step 3: Wait for Review
**Processing Timeline**:
- ⏱️ Applications reviewed within 24-48 hours
- 📧 You'll receive notification of status
- ❓ Officers may message you with questions
- ✅ Acceptance or rejection notified

**What Officers Look For**:
- [ ] Meets minimum level requirement
- [ ] Respectful communication in application
- [ ] No red flags in player history
- [ ] Matches alliance values
- [ ] Available time commitment

#### Step 4: Acceptance
**You've Been Accepted!**
1. Status changes to "Approved"
2. You can now join alliance
3. Click "Join Alliance"
4. Receive welcome message
5. Access alliance chat

#### Step 5: Onboarding
See [Onboarding Checklist](#onboarding-checklist) below

## New Member Program

### Structured Support

We want your first month to be successful! That's why we offer:

### Buddy System

**Your Mentor**:
- Assigned within first week
- Available for questions
- Plays with you in squads
- Helps with game mechanics
- Introduces you to members

**Your Buddy's Role**:
- [ ] Schedule first group play session
- [ ] Explain alliance culture
- [ ] Help optimize your loadout
- [ ] Teach community tactics
- [ ] Give constructive feedback

**How to Connect**:
1. Officers introduce you to buddy
2. Add buddy to friends list
3. Message buddy to schedule time
4. Ask them anything!

### Learning Events

**Beginner Training**:
- **Frequency**: Weekly (Mondays 6 PM)
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours
- **Focus**: Basic squad tactics
- **Instructor**: Senior members
- **Attendance**: Optional but recommended

**Strategy Sessions**:
- **Frequency**: Bi-weekly (Wednesdays 7 PM)
- **Duration**: 1.5 hours
- **Focus**: Advanced tactics, weapon optimization
- **Instructor**: Officers
- **Attendance**: Optional

**New Member Events**:
- **Frequency**: Twice per month
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Focus**: Fun and connection
- **Instructor**: Event coordinator
- **Attendance**: Highly encouraged

### Probation & Promotion

**New Member Status** (First 2 weeks):
- Active learning phase
- Limited event participation
- Full chat access
- Supervised gameplay

**Transition to Active Member**:
- After 2 weeks + positive feedback
- Officer review of conduct
- Automatic if no violations
- Full event access

**Promotion Criteria**:
- ✅ Read and follow all rules
- ✅ Participated in 2+ events
- ✅ No conduct violations
- ✅ Showed positive attitude
- ✅ Accepted by current members

## FAQ

### Application Questions

**Q: How long does the application take to review?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours. You'll be notified by message in-game.

**Q: What if my application is rejected?**
A: You can usually reapply after 2 weeks. Ask an officer why for feedback.

**Q: Can I apply if I'm below level 10?**
A: Not initially, but you can join once you level up and reapply.

**Q: Do I need to be good at the game?**
A: No! We welcome all skill levels. We'll help you improve.

### Membership Questions

**Q: What if I can't play 2-3 times per week?**
A: Talk to leadership. We offer "Reserves" status for people with limited availability.

**Q: Can I leave and rejoin later?**
A: Yes! You can rejoin anytime (no penalty). Just reapply.

**Q: What if I'm from a different country/timezone?**
A: No problem! We have international members. We schedule events for multiple timezones.

**Q: Do I have to spend real money?**
A: Absolutely not. Everything is earned through gameplay.

### Activity Questions

**Q: What if I get busy and can't play?**
A: Just let leadership know! We have options:
- Temporary leave (keep slot)
- Reduced participation
- Reserves status

**Q: How often should I attend events?**
A: Participate when you can. We ask for 2-3 times/week on average, but life happens!

**Q: What's the most time-intensive activity?**
A: Tournaments and raids (usually 1-3 hours). Regular squad play is flexible.

### Advancement Questions

**Q: When can I become an officer?**
A: Typically after 3+ months, if nominated and approved by leadership.

**Q: Is there a level cap for participation?**
A: No! Members of any level can participate in events (events scale to levels).

**Q: How do I improve faster?**
A: Get a mentor, watch experienced players, practice regularly, ask for feedback.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Does my game version need to be updated?**
A: Yes, keep your game fully updated. We need consistent versions for compatibility.

**Q: Do I need a microphone for voice chat?**
A: Recommended but not required. Text chat is available too.

**Q: What if my game crashes during an event?**
A: Just rejoin! There's no penalty. Let squad know.
