## 👑 Alliance Leader

**Role Overview:**  
The Alliance Leader provides overall direction and makes major decisions for the alliance.

### Responsibilities

- 🎯 **Vision & Strategy** - Set alliance direction and goals
- ⚖️ **Final Decisions** - Approve major policy and leadership decisions
- 👮 **Conflict Resolution** - Mediate serious disputes
- 📢 **Communication** - Major announcements and direction
- 👥 **Leadership Team** - Recruit and manage officers
- 📋 **Documentation** - Ensure charter and rules are followed
- 🤝 **Community** - Represent alliance to broader community
- 💡 **Innovation** - Suggest improvements and new initiatives

### How to Reach the Leader

- Message in-game (priority)
- Ask an officer to forward concerns
- Create formal report through channels
- Serious issues only (not general questions)

### Leader Standards

- ✅ Model all alliance values
- ✅ Fair and unbiased decisions
- ✅ Accessible to members
- ✅ Transparent reasoning
- ✅ Willing to admit mistakes
- ✅ Long-term vision for alliance

## 🛡️ Officer Team

**Team Size:** 3-4 officers (including coordinators)

**Overall Role:**  
Officers support the Alliance Leader, manage specific areas of the alliance, and ensure smooth operations.

### General Officer Duties

- 👥 Support leader with decisions
- 📣 Communicate with members
- 🎯 Manage assigned areas
- 📋 Enforce code of conduct
- 🤝 Welcome new members
- 💪 Model alliance values
- 🔄 Report issues to leadership
- 📊 Track metrics/progress

### Officer Types

#### Event Coordinator
**Focuses on:** Planning and running events

**Responsibilities:**
- 📅 Schedule regular events
- 👥 Organize raid groups
- 📢 Announce upcoming events
- 🎯 Track sign-ups and attendance
- 🏆 Run tournaments and competitions
- 🤝 Get feedback on events
- 📝 Record event results

**Who to contact:**
- Questions about upcoming events
- Want to suggest an event type
- Need help signing up
- Had a great event experience to share

#### Recruitment Officer
**Focuses on:** Bringing quality members into the alliance

**Responsibilities:**
- ✅ Review applications
- 📋 Communicate with applicants
- 👋 Welcome new members
- 🎓 Help with onboarding
- 📊 Track member retention
- 🔍 Monitor for activity
- 💬 Gather feedback

**Who to contact:**
- Questions about the application process
- Have a friend wanting to join
- Want to suggest recruitment ideas
- Feedback on onboarding experience

#### Treasury Officer
**Focuses on:** Managing alliance resources

**Responsibilities:**
- 💰 Manage alliance bank/resources
- 📊 Track inventory
- 🔄 Distribute to needy members
- 🎖️ Manage rewards
- 📈 Plan resource gathering
- 💳 Handle trades
- 📋 Keep records

**Who to contact:**
- Need items from alliance bank
- Want to donate resources
- Questions about alliance wealth
- Suggestions for resource management

### Becoming an Officer

**Qualifications:**
- ✅ Active member (3+ months)
- ✅ Consistently shows alliance values
- ✅ Respected by community
- ✅ Interested and willing
- ✅ Leadership potential
- ✅ Time available for role

**Process:**
1. Leader identifies potential candidates
2. Discusses with current officer team
3. Invites conversation with candidate
4. Candidate decides if interested
5. Formal announcement if accepted
6. Training and onboarding

**Officer Responsibilities:**
- Regular availability (evenings/weekends)
- Active event participation
- Responsive to communications
- Fair and consistent
- Community-focused
- Continuous improvement

## 🎯 Decision-Making Process

### Everyday Decisions

**What:** Event times, announcements, minor tweaks  
**Who decides:** Relevant officer  
**Timeline:** Immediate  
**Communication:** In-game chat

**Example:**
- "Friday raid starts at 7 PM"
- "New member Sarah is approved"
- "Alliance bank currently has 500 stone"

### Important Decisions

**What:** Rule changes, major events, membership changes  
**Who decides:** Officers meet + leader approves  
**Timeline:** 24-48 hours  
**Communication:** Formal announcement

**Example:**
- "We're changing the activity requirement to 3x/week"
- "First-time member gets XP boost"
- "New raid difficulty level available"

### Major Decisions

**What:** Fundamental alliance changes, serious issues, charter amendments  
**Who decides:** All officers + leader discussion  
**Timeline:** 3-7 days  
**Communication:** Full community discussion

**Example:**
- "Changing our core values"
- "Major member dispute resolution"
- "Alliance restructuring"
- "New charter amendment"

### Member Input

**How members influence decisions:**
- 💬 Voice opinions in chat
- 📝 Suggest improvements
- 🗳️ Vote on important issues (when applicable)
- 📧 Message officers with concerns
- 💡 Share feedback regularly

## 🔄 Meeting & Communication

### Regular Meetings

**Officer Meetings:**
- Frequency: Monthly (or as needed)
- Purpose: Discuss alliance issues, plan events
- Attendance: Officers + Leader

**Town Hall Meetings:**
- Frequency: Quarterly (every 3 months)
- Purpose: Share updates, gather community input
- Attendance: All members welcome

### Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Frequency |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Alliance Chat** | Daily communication | Real-time |
| **Announcements** | Important news | As needed |
| **Event Posts** | Event organization | Weekly+ |
| **Officer Meetings** | Leadership | Monthly |
| **Town Halls** | Community | Quarterly |

## 🤝 Succession Planning

### Leader Succession

**If leader needs to step down:**
1. Leader discusses with officers
2. Officers vote on successor
3. New leader takes position
4. Announcement to community
5. Transition period if needed

**Successor qualities:**
- Trusted by community
- Agrees with alliance values
- Available and committed
- Leadership experience
- Community support

### Officer Succession

**If officer needs to step down:**
1. Officer informs leader
2. Leader identifies replacement
3. New officer trained quickly
4. Announcement to community
5. Smooth transition

## ❓ FAQ: Structure & Organization

**Q: How do I become an officer?**  
A: Be an active, respected member for 3+ months. Show leadership potential. Officers can nominate you or you can express interest.

**Q: Can I talk to the Alliance Leader directly?**  
A: Yes, for serious issues. Usually, talk to your relevant officer first who can escalate if needed.

**Q: How are decisions actually made?**  
A: Officers discuss, leader makes final call. For major changes, full officer team involved. Members' opinions matter!

**Q: What if I disagree with a decision?**  
A: Tell an officer! Constructive feedback is always welcome. We may not change it, but we'll explain our reasoning.

**Q: How often do officers meet?**  
A: Officially monthly, but communicate constantly. Issues get addressed as they come up.

**Q: Can members suggest policy changes?**  
A: Absolutely! Talk to any officer or mention in chat. Good ideas help us improve.

**Q: Is there a waiting list for officer roles?**  
A: No formal list, but interested members should talk to leadership. We're always watching for leadership potential.

**Q: What if an officer isn't doing their job well?**  
A: Members can provide feedback. Leader and other officers address performance issues. Our standards are high!
