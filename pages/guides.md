## 🎮 Gameplay Guides

### Beginner's Guide

#### Getting Started
1. **Complete the tutorial** - Learn basic controls and mechanics
2. **Manage your inventory** - Carry important supplies
3. **Learn survival basics** - Food, water, health management
4. **Find shelter** - Understand safe zones and bases
5. **Practice combat** - Use training modes before real combat

#### Key Beginner Tips
- 🎯 Focus on survival first, combat second
- 💡 Pay attention to environmental hazards
- 📍 Learn map landmarks for navigation
- 🛠️ Understand crafting basics early
- 📚 Read in-game tutorials completely

#### First Week Goals
- Reach level 5 safely
- Learn basic combat mechanics
- Build your first base
- Complete 5+ survival missions
- Join team activities

---

### Combat Tips

#### Individual Combat
- **Aim for weak points** - Headshots deal more damage
- **Use cover effectively** - Don't stand exposed
- **Manage ammo** - Pick up supplies regularly
- **Learn enemy patterns** - Predict their movements
- **Stay mobile** - Avoid becoming stationary targets
- **Use consumables** - Heal potions, buffs help survival

#### Team Combat
- **Stay near teammates** - Avoid being isolated
- **Communicate positions** - Tell team where enemies are
- **Focus fire** - All attack same target = faster kills
- **Cover each other** - When one person attacks, other covers
- **Revive fallen** - Help teammates get back in action
- **Manage cooldowns** - Coordinate ability usage

#### Combat Positioning
```
Better positioning strategies:
✓ High ground advantage
✓ Use walls and objects for cover
✓ Spread out (not bunched together)
✓ Face enemies with backup nearby
✗ Don't fight alone
✗ Don't stand in open
✗ Don't wander from team
```

|------|---------|--------|----------|

| **Healing Potions** | Restore health | Uncommon | High |

- Keep crafting materials organized

### Building & Base Setup

#### Base Location Tips
- 📍 Choose defensible location
- 🌳 Near resources (water, trees, stone)
- 👀 Good visibility for spotting threats
- 🚪 Multiple entry/exit points
- ⚠️ Avoid obvious/popular locations

#### Basic Base Layout
```
        Main Gate
          ▼
    ┌─────────────┐
    │    Outer    │
    │ Walls/Gate  │
    └──────┬──────┘
           │
    ┌──────────────┐
    │   Main Area  │
    │  Crafting    │
    │  Sleeping    │
    └──────┬───────┘
           │
    ┌──────────────┐
    │   Storage    │
    │   (Secure)   │
    └──────────────┘
```

#### Upgrades Priority
1. **Walls & Defense** - Keep enemies out
2. **Doors & Gates** - Control access
3. **Storage** - Secure your items
4. **Crafting Area** - Make equipment
5. **Living Space** - Comfort (last priority)

## 🎯 Combat Strategies

### Raid Preparation

#### Before a Raid
- ✅ Full inventory of supplies
- ✅ All gear repaired and ready
- ✅ Healing items stocked
- ✅ Ammo/consumables for 2+ hours
- ✅ Understand raid objective
- ✅ Know team composition
- ✅ Practice if it's a new challenge

#### Raid Objective Types

**Survival Raid:**
- Last X minutes without dying
- Waves of increasing difficulty
- Focus: Survive, not conquer

**Elimination Raid:**
- Defeat all enemies in zone
- Boss fights included
- Focus: Damage, coordination

**Resource Raid:**
- Collect X resources from location
- Avoid/defeat obstacles
- Focus: Efficiency, speed

**Defense Raid:**
- Protect location from waves
- Enemies come in progressively
- Focus: Defense, organization

#### Raid Roles

| Role | Responsibilities | Equipment |
|------|------------------|-----------|
| **Tank** | Take damage, draw attention | Heavy armor, shields |
| **Damage** | Deal heavy damage to enemies | High-damage weapons |
| **Support** | Heal team, provide buffs | Healing items, buffs |
| **Control** | Crowd control, positioning | Crowd control abilities |

### Team Coordination

#### Communication During Combat
- 🎤 Use voice if available
- 📣 Call out enemy positions ("Enemy left, 2 o'clock!")
- ⚠️ Alert team to hazards
- ✅ Confirm objectives clearly
- 📍 Share target focus ("Focusing left enemy!")

#### Coordination Signals
```
Combat Callouts:
Left / Center / Right
High / Mid / Low
Near / Medium / Far
Flanking / Retreating / Holding
```

#### Team Formations

**Defensive Line:**
- Stand side by side
- Good for area control
- Vulnerable to flanking

**Wedge Formation:**
- Point forward, spreading back
- Good for charging forward
- Protects flanks

**Scattered:**
- Spread out with spacing
- Harder to hit all at once
- Better for mobility

**Layered:**
- Front line tanks, back line damage
- Maximum protection for damage
- Harder to coordinate

## 📅 Alliance Event Types

### Raid Events

**What:** Coordinated group challenge  
**Difficulty:** Normal → Hard → Expert  
**Duration:** 30 min - 2 hours  
**Preparation:** 1-2 days notice  
**Rewards:** Loot, XP, alliance points

**How to Participate:**
1. Check event announcement
2. Sign up in alliance chat
3. Prepare your gear
4. Log in on time
5. Coordinate with team
6. Complete objective

### PvP Tournaments

**What:** Player vs Player competitions  
**Format:** 1v1 or team battles  
**Schedule:** Monthly events  
**Prizes:** Recognition, items  

**Tournament Rules:**
- Fair play and sportsmanship
- Standard gear only (no exploit combos)
- Best of 3 matches
- Spectators welcome

### Farming Events

**What:** Group resource gathering  
**Locations:** Multiple zones  
**Duration:** 1-2 hours  
**Goal:** Collect X resources together

**Benefits:**
- Safety in numbers
- Faster completion
- Fun community time
- Shared resources

### Social Events

**What:** Casual community hangouts  
**Types:** Party raids, training, socializing  
**No pressure, just fun!

### Leveling Strategy

**Early Game (Levels 1-10):**
- Focus: Survival and basics
- Goal: Learn mechanics
- Activity: Complete tutorials, solo missions
- Equipment: Starter gear

**Mid Game (Levels 10-30):**
- Focus: Skill building and teaming
- Goal: Prepare for group content

**Late Game (Levels 30+):**
- Focus: Optimization and leadership
- Goal: Contribute to alliance

### Class Specialization

Pick a role to specialize in:

**Warrior (Tank):**
- High health and armor
- Defensive abilities
- Support role in combat
- Learn defensive skills

**Rogue (Damage):**
- High damage output
- Stealth and mobility
- Glass cannon (low defense)
- Learn offensive skills

**Mage (Support):**
- Healing and buffs
- Crowd control
- Medium stats across board
- Learn support skills

**Archer (Ranged):**
- Moderate damage
- Good range and positioning

- Learn precision skills

## ❓ FAQ: Guides & Strategies

### Gameplay

**Q: What's the best way to level up?**  
A: Balance missions, group events, and practicing skills. Consistency beats grinding.

**Q: When should I upgrade my gear?**  
A: Upgrade when you notice difficulty spiking, or every 5-10 levels.

**Q: What class should I pick?**  
A: Pick what appeals to you! Different classes fill different roles.

**Q: Should I focus on PvE or PvP?**  
A: Both are valuable. Try both and see what you enjoy!

### Resources

**Q: How do I get rare materials?**  
A: Group farming, boss drops, trading with members, or challenging content.

**Q: Should I save resources or use them?**  
A: Use them! Sitting on supplies doesn't help. Use what you need to progress.

**Q: How do I get better gear fast?**  
A: Join raids with drops, save currency for vendors, or craft upgrades.

### Combat

**Q: What if I keep dying?**  
A: Check your gear, learn enemy patterns, stay with team, practice positioning.

**Q: Am I too weak for this raid?**  
A: Difficulty spikes are normal. Upgrade gear, practice, or try easier version first.

**Q: How do I improve at PvP?**  
A: Practice against NPCs, watch experienced players, duel friends, learn maps.

**Q: What if my team keeps losing?**  
A: Analyze what went wrong, communicate better, adjust strategy, try different roles.

### Raids & Events

**Q: Do I have to participate in events?**  
A: No, events are optional. But they're great for fun, rewards, and teamwork!

**Q: What if I can't make an event?**  
A: Let the organizer know! There will be other events.

**Q: What if I perform poorly in an event?**  
A: No judgment! We're here to learn. Ask for tips from experienced members.

**Q: Can I join mid-event?**  
A: Sometimes, but usually events have set teams. Join the next one!

### Strategies

**Q: Is there a "best" strategy?**  
A: It depends on the challenge! Adapt to circumstances and team composition.

**Q: Should I specialize or generalize?**  
A: Specialize gives you depth; generalize gives flexibility. Both are valuable.

**Q: How do I contribute more to the alliance?**  
A: Participate regularly, help newer members, lead when asked, suggest improvements.
