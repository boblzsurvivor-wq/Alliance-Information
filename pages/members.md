# 👥 Member Information

Everything you need to know about joining our alliance, member tiers, expectations, and resources available to you.

---

## 📋 How to Join

Joining is simple! Follow these 6 steps.

### Step 1: Check Requirements ✅

Before applying, make sure you meet these requirements:

- ✅ **Level 10+** in Last Z Survival Shooter
- ✅ **Read the Alliance Charter** (takes ~15 minutes)
- ✅ **Understand Code of Conduct** (we're respectful here)
- ✅ **Commit to participation** (2-3 times per week)
- ✅ **Have a positive attitude** (no toxic behavior)
- ✅ **Be 13+** (age requirement)

**Not level 10 yet?** No problem! Work on leveling up and come back when ready.

### Step 2: Read Our Charter 📖

Take time to read the [Alliance Charter](charter.md). It covers:
- Our core values (Respect, Collaboration, Growth, Integrity, Fun)
- Our code of conduct
- How we operate

This isn't a punishment - it's about making sure we're a good fit for each other!

### Step 3: Prepare Your Application 📝

Think about what to say when applying. You'll want to mention:
- Your current level
- Why you want to join
- What you enjoy about the game
- Anything else about yourself (optional)

**Example:**
*"Hi! I'm level 15 and looking for a friendly community to play with. I enjoy both PvE raids and casual gaming. I read your charter and love your values. Hope to play with you!"*

### Step 4: Search & Submit Application 🎮

In-game:
1. Open the Alliance menu
2. Search for our alliance name
3. Read the brief description
4. Click **"Request to Join"**
5. Write your message (see Step 3)
6. Submit!

### Step 5: Wait for Approval ⏱️

Our Recruitment Officer will review your application within 24-48 hours.

**What we're looking for:**
- ✅ Meeting minimum level
- ✅ Thoughtful application message
- ✅ Good standing in other groups (if visible)
- ✅ Genuine interest in our values

**What might delay approval:**
- ⏳ Recent bans or violations elsewhere
- ⏳ Concerning application message
- ⏳ Need to verify information

### Step 6: Get Approved & Join! 🎉

**You're in!** Now:

1. **Say hello** in alliance chat
2. **Check pinned messages** for info
3. **Read the New Member Checklist** (below)
4. **Introduce yourself** - Tell us about you!
5. **Start participating!** Join events or just hang out

## 🎖️ Membership Tiers

Our alliance has clear membership tiers with different responsibilities and status.

### Tier 1: Applicant
**Status:** Awaiting approval

**Duration:** 24-48 hours

**Requirements:**
- Meet minimum requirements
- Submit application

**What You Can Do:**
- Read public information
- Submit application
- Wait for decision

**What You Get:**
- Fair review
- Quick decision
- Feedback if rejected

### Tier 2: New Member
**Status:** Recently joined, learning

**Duration:** 2 weeks

**Requirements:**
- Application approved
- Active in alliance
- Be respectful

**What You Can Do:**
- Participate in events
- Chat with community
- Ask questions
- Use alliance resources
- Make friends

**What You Get:**
- Warm welcome
- Mentoring from officers
- Support and guidance
- Full event access
- Sense of belonging

**Automatic Transition:**
After 2 weeks, you automatically become an **Active Member**!

### Tier 3: Active Member
**Status:** Core member, full participation

**Duration:** Ongoing

**Requirements:**
- Completed 2+ weeks
- Regular participation
- Following code of conduct
- Positive attitude

**What You Can Do:**
- Lead events (if interested)
- Mentor new members
- Vote on important decisions
- Suggest improvements
- Hold officer roles (if interested)

**What You Get:**
- Full member rights
- Voice in community
- Recognition opportunities
- Leadership potential
- Deep friendships

**Officer Track:**
If you show leadership potential, you can be nominated for officer positions.

### Tier 4: Officer (Optional)
**Status:** Leadership position

**Duration:** As long as you hold the role

**Requirements:**
- 3+ months as active member
- Shown leadership skills
- Community respect
- Willingness to lead
- Available for role

**Roles:**
- Event Coordinator
- Recruitment Officer
- Treasury Officer
- Or other as needed

**What You Do:**
- Lead specific areas
- Make decisions
- Support the community
- Model alliance values
- Guide new members

**What You Get:**
- Leadership experience
- Community recognition
- Decision-making authority
- Officer perks/status
- Sense of purpose

## 👥 Member Expectations

### Conduct Expectations

**Be Respectful:**
- Treat everyone with dignity
- Use kind language
- Respect different playstyles
- Accept feedback gracefully
- No toxic behavior

**Be Collaborative:**
- Work with teammates
- Share resources when reasonable
- Communicate clearly
- Support others
- Celebrate together

**Be Honest:**
- Be truthful in interactions
- Play fairly (no cheating)
- Follow through on commitments
- Admit mistakes
- Act with integrity

**Be Fun:**
- Enjoy the game
- Don't take things too seriously
- Laugh with others
- Share positive energy
- Have a good attitude

### Participation Expectations

**Frequency:**
- Target: 2-3 times per week
- Minimum: 1+ times per week to stay active
- Life happens - communicate if busy
- No judgment for real-life priorities

**Events:**
- Sign up if you'll attend
- Show up on time
- Give advance notice if you can't make it
- Participate fully when you're there

**Communication:**
- Check alliance chat regularly
- Read announcements
- Respond to messages
- Ask for help when needed
- Help others when you can

**Community:**
- Welcome new members
- Share tips and strategies
- Celebrate good moments
- Report problems respectfully
- Contribute to positive culture

### What NOT to Do

**Don't:**
- ❌ Cheat or exploit
- ❌ Be mean or toxic
- ❌ Hoard resources selfishly
- ❌ Abandon events you signed up for
- ❌ Bully or harass anyone
- ❌ Spread rumors or gossip
- ❌ Break game rules
- ❌ Disrespect leadership

**Minor Violations:**
- Friendly reminder from officers
- Conversation to clarify rules
- Support to improve

**Serious Violations:**
- Investigation
- Formal discussion
- Possible probation
- Potential removal (in extreme cases)

## ❓ FAQ

### Joining Process

**Q: Is level 10 a hard requirement?**  
A: Yes, for game balance. Work on leveling and come back when ready!

**Q: What if I've been banned elsewhere?**  
A: We look at context. Recent bans might delay approval, but old history doesn't disqualify. Apply and be honest.

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Usually 24-48 hours. We try to be quick! You'll get a message either way.

**Q: Can I be rejected?**  
A: Rarely. We accept most applicants. Rejection would be for red flags or dishonesty.

**Q: Can I appeal if rejected?**  
A: Yes! Ask why and what you can do differently. Give it time and reapply later.

**Q: Do I need to be good at the game?**  
A: No! We accept all skill levels. Attitude matters more than skill.

**Q: What if I'm not sure about joining?**  
A: Read the charter first. If you have questions, message an officer!

### Membership

**Q: What if I can't play 2-3x per week?**  
A: That's your target, not a hard rule. Life happens! Just let us know if you'll be gone.

**Q: What if I take a break?**  
A: Let an officer know. You can come back whenever! We'll save your spot.

**Q: Can I leave and rejoin?**  
A: Yes. You can leave anytime, and you're welcome back later.

**Q: What's the time commitment?**  
A: Events vary (30 min - 2 hours). Chat is optional. No pressure!

**Q: Will I be kicked for inactivity?**  
A: Only after 4+ weeks with no communication. We give warnings first.

**Q: Can I be an officer?**  
A: Maybe! Show leadership, stick around 3+ months, and express interest.

**Q: What if I don't want to be an officer?**  
A: That's fine! Regular members are just as valued.

### Rules & Conduct

**Q: What if I break a rule by mistake?**  
A: No big deal. Officers will explain and help you improve.

**Q: What's the worst that happens?**  
A: For serious violations, you could be removed. But we always give chances first.

**Q: Can I be banned permanently?**  
A: Extremely rare. Only for serious real-world harm or repeated severe violations.

**Q: What if I disagree with a rule?**  
A: Tell leadership! We welcome feedback and can discuss changes.

**Q: Can I appeal a decision?**  
A: Yes! You have 2 weeks to appeal any consequence.

### Resources & Support

**Q: Where do I find game guides?**  
A: Check [Guides & Resources](guides.md) or ask in chat!

**Q: Can officers help me improve?**  
A: Yes! Officers mentor and help all members improve.

**Q: What if I have a problem with someone?**  
A: Tell an officer privately. We'll help mediate fairly.

**Q: Is it safe here?**  
A: Yes. We enforce anti-harassment policies strictly.

**Q: What if I'm new to the game?**  
A: We welcome new players! People will help you learn.

### Other Questions

**Q: Is there a Discord?**  
A: That's handled separately from the GitHub site. Ask an officer in-game!

**Q: Can I bring a friend?**  
A: Yes! Have them read the charter and apply!

**Q: Are there fees or costs?**  
A: No! The alliance is free to join and participate in.

**Q: What's the size limit?**  
A: We accept new members as long as we can maintain community quality.

**Q: Can we have multiple alliances?**  
A: Game rules determine that. Ask your game admins!
