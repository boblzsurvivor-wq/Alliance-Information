# 📜 Alliance Charter

The official founding document of our alliance. This charter outlines our mission, core values, code of conduct, and governance principles.

---

## 🎯 Mission Statement

**"To build a thriving community of Last Z Survival Shooter players committed to cooperation, mutual growth, and creating an inclusive, respectful environment where every member can enjoy the game and forge meaningful connections."**

In simpler terms: We're here to have fun together, help each other get better, and be kind to one another.

## 💎 Core Values

Our alliance is built on five core values that guide everything we do:

### 🤝 **Respect**

We value and respect every member as an individual.

**What this means:**
- Treat everyone with dignity
- Listen to different opinions
- Accept people's differences
- No bullying, harassment, or discrimination
- Value contributions from all members
- Acknowledge others' worth and effort

**In Practice:**
- Use kind language
- Don't make fun of others' abilities
- Include people regardless of skill level
- Listen more than you talk sometimes
- Apologize when you're wrong

### 🎮 **Collaboration**

We're stronger together than apart. Teamwork is at our heart.

**What this means:**
- Work together toward common goals
- Share knowledge and resources
- Support teammates in success
- Communicate openly
- Make decisions together
- Help others succeed

**In Practice:**
- Coordinate in team battles
- Share tips and strategies
- Mentor new members
- Help complete group objectives
- Celebrate team wins together
- Contribute your skills

### 📈 **Growth**

We believe in continuous improvement and helping others improve.

**What this means:**
- Everyone has room to grow
- Mistakes are learning opportunities
- Support others' development
- Share knowledge and experience
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Challenge ourselves positively

**In Practice:**
- Encourage members to try new things
- Share lessons you've learned
- Practice and improve skills
- Ask for help when learning
- Congratulate progress
- Foster a growth mindset

### ✨ **Integrity**

We play fairly and act honestly in everything we do.

**What this means:**
- No cheating or exploiting
- Be truthful in interactions
- Keep your commitments
- Follow game rules
- Act ethically
- Lead by example

**In Practice:**
- Play by the rules
- Be honest about your abilities
- Show up when you say you will
- Report problems fairly
- Admit mistakes
- Do the right thing even when no one's watching

### 🎉 **Fun**

It's a game! We remember to enjoy it and have fun together.

**What this means:**
- Gaming should be enjoyable
- Don't take things too seriously
- Celebrate good moments
- Laugh with others (not at them)
- Create positive energy
- Enjoy the social experience

**In Practice:**
- Celebrate victories
- Laugh at funny moments
- Be positive in chat
- Create a welcoming atmosphere
- Support morale
- Enjoy the friendships you make

## ⚖️ Enforcement & Consequences

### Progressive Discipline

We believe in fairness and giving people chances to improve:

**Level 1: Gentle Reminder**
- **When:** First small violation
- **Action:** Private message from officer
- **What:** Explain the issue, remind of values
- **Outcome:** Clarify expectations, move forward
- **Example:** New member doesn't understand chat etiquette

**Level 2: Formal Warning**
- **When:** Repeated minor violation or moderate offense
- **Action:** Formal conversation with officer
- **What:** Document the issue, explain consequences
- **Outcome:** Member must improve, consequences for repeat
- **Example:** Member frequently complains, after reminder continues

**Level 3: Probation**
- **When:** Serious violation or multiple warnings unheeded
- **Action:** Member placed on probation (1-4 weeks)
- **What:** Restrictions on certain activities, close monitoring
- **Outcome:** Member must show improvement during probation
- **Example:** Member was rude in multiple conversations

**Level 4: Suspension**
- **When:** Extreme behavior or continued violations during probation
- **Action:** Temporary removal from alliance (1-8 weeks)
- **What:** Cannot participate, can appeal before return
- **Outcome:** Return after suspension if conditions met
- **Example:** Member engaged in harassment despite warnings

**Level 5: Removal**
- **When:** Severe harm or repeated extreme violations
- **Action:** Permanent removal from alliance
- **What:** Banned permanently, cannot rejoin
- **Outcome:** Member status terminated
- **Example:** Serious illegal activity, repeated harassment

### Appeal Process

**Members can appeal any discipline decision:**

1. **Request Appeal** - Write to Alliance Leader within 2 weeks
2. **Present Case** - Explain your perspective
3. **Review** - Leadership discusses with you
4. **Decision** - Leadership makes final call with explanation
5. **Outcome** - Consequence upheld, reduced, or removed

**Appeals are taken seriously.** We want fair outcomes for everyone.

## 🏛️ Governance Principles

### How We Make Decisions

**Three Levels of Decisions:**

**Everyday Decisions** (Officer level)
- Event times, minor announcements
- Single officer decides
- Quick (immediate)

**Important Decisions** (Officer team)
- Rule changes, major events
- Officers discuss, leader approves
- Medium (24-48 hours)

**Major Decisions** (Full community)
- Charter changes, serious issues
- Officers discuss, community input, leader decides
- Longer (3-7 days)

### Member Voice

Members have influence in several ways:

- 💬 **Chat discussions** - Officers listen and respond
- 🗳️ **Votes on major issues** - Members get to weigh in
- 💡 **Suggestions** - Always welcome and considered
- 📝 **Feedback** - Regularly solicited and reviewed
- 🎯 **Concerns** - Addressed fairly and professionally

### Leadership Accountability

Our leaders are accountable to members:

- ✅ Decisions must follow charter
- ✅ Transparency about reasoning
- ✅ Open to feedback and criticism
- ✅ Fair and unbiased treatment
- ✅ Willing to admit and correct mistakes
- ✅ Regular communication with members
- ✅ Accessible for concerns

## 📝 Charter Amendments

### How to Suggest Changes

**Process:**
1. Discuss in alliance chat
2. Get feedback from community
3. Submit formal suggestion to leadership
4. Leadership reviews and considers
5. Community discussion period
6. Official vote if major change
7. Implementation if approved

### Charter Rights

**Members have the right to:**
- Suggest charter amendments
- Request official discussion
- Vote on major changes
- Appeal unfair decisions
- Know exactly what rules are

**Charter can only be changed:**
- With broad agreement (usually 70%+)
- With proper process and notice
- Without removing fundamental values
- Through democratic means

## 🙋 FAQ

### About the Charter

**Q: Do I have to agree with every value?**  
A: You should support the alliance mission. Disagreement on specifics is okay—discussion is encouraged.

**Q: Can the charter change?**  
A: Yes, through a democratic process. Core values won't change lightly, but we adapt as needed.

**Q: What if I don't like a rule?**  
A: Tell leadership! We take feedback seriously. Explain your concern—you might convince us to change it.

**Q: Is the charter legally binding?**  
A: It's our community agreement. Breaking it can lead to removal, but it's not a legal contract.

**Q: Who enforces the charter?**  
A: Officers enforce it. Alliance Leader makes final calls on serious issues.

### About Values

**Q: What if someone breaks a value?**  
A: They'll be reminded and given chance to improve. Repeated violations have consequences.

**Q: Do officers follow the same rules?**  
A: Yes! Officers are held to an even higher standard.

**Q: What if an officer breaks the code?**  
A: Leadership investigates and takes action, up to removal from officer role.

**Q: Can I call out an officer?**  
A: Yes! Report through proper channels. We take this seriously.

### About Consequences

**Q: Will I be kicked for one mistake?**  
A: No. We give warnings first. Immediate removal is only for extreme situations.

**Q: Can I come back after suspension?**  
A: Yes! Most suspensions are temporary. Follow the conditions and you can return.

**Q: Is removal permanent?**  
A: Usually yes, but in rare cases the Alliance Leader might allow return after long period.

**Q: Can I appeal a removal?**  
A: You can request reconsideration, but if the leadership stands firm, removal sticks.

### About Fairness

**Q: Is enforcement fair?**  
A: We try our absolute best. Different situations may have different outcomes, but we're consistent.

**Q: What if I think I was treated unfairly?**  
A: Appeal! We review the situation and will admit if we were wrong.

**Q: Are officers treated differently?**  
A: Officers are held to a higher standard, not a lower one.

**Q: How do I report a problem?**  
A: Message an officer privately or contact the Alliance Leader.
